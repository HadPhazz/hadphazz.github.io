This project, done using Python and PyGame, took me around one month and a half. It's a simple game based on the original TRON movie, where two players have to drive again eachother. The winner is the one who trap the other with the light trail comming off his motorbike.

The challenge was to deal with the trails, as the other stuff was just putting textures and sprites on the screen. But it was a nice way to learn more on PyGame.
