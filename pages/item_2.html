This Raspberry Pi is a custom one, with a POE hat to power it by a POE swith or injector, and a custom 3D printed lid. The lid took 1h to print, and I can fix it to a container, which my plan is to but it in the water (crazy idea yeah).

I'm running a Razer Kiyo webcam with it, as I can record everything from my window with it (or under water hehe).
