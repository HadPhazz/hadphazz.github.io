This case is made of wood, acrylic and 3D printed side panels with a honeycomb pattern. It's a 180x180mm design, with a mITX motherboard inside.

I really like the design even if the conception of this case is not perfect, there are some gaps in the corners, and it was a real pain to cut the acrylic !
