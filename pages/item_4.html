This project is my first computer build, using an ASUS N3050T Thin Mini ITX Motherboard, with an Intel® Celeron® Brasswell Dual-Core N3050 SoC onboard, running at 1.60Ghz, up to 2.16Ghz turbo.
I also have 2 sticks of 2GB SODIMM DDR3L Memory from Hnyx, a WiFi card from an old ASUS laptop, and a 120Gb mSATA SSD from Yucun.
At the back of the frame, there is a DVD reader that I took apart and put on the wood with some double side tape, it's working good, you just have to whatch out for your fingers !
And for the case, I used some oak planks which my uncle gave me, some screws and a bit of imagination, as I wanted an open bench where we could see the motherboard and the beautiful golden AiO.
The total cost of this project was slighty less than 120€, which is great, considering that I had most of the components from scrap recovery of old laptops.
